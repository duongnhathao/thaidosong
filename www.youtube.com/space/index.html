FAQs

General Pop-ups For brands

  • Is YouTube Space open to the public?

    As a global precaution to best protect the health and safety of our Creators and employees, our YouTube Space facilities have been temporarily closed until further notice. We apologize for this inconvenience, and did not make this decision lightly. We are closely following the situation and will provide updates on our site as soon as anything changes. In the meantime, we are offering virtual workshops and events around the world for you to sign-up for through our events calendar page.

  • How do I Unlock the Space?

    Unlock the Space is our orientation that grants you access to book a stage and gear for your next project at a YouTube Space. However, due to the current global health crisis, our facilities are temporarily closed. As a result, we are not currently running this orientation for any of our locations. In the meantime, we encourage you to visit our events calendar page to sign-up for any of the global virtual workshops and events we're currently offering to eligible partners.

  • How much does it cost to use YouTube Space?

    Nothing. Zip. Zero. We provide just about everything you’ll need to make your next big video here including equipment, studio space, post production facilities, and even trainings and workshops. All at no cost for you.

  • Will I be able to get production crew support?

    Our team will be on hand to ensure you have a great experience, but you'll need to bring your own production crew and support.

  • Are there certain regulations around what I can shoot at YouTube Space?

    Yep! Content must meet YouTube’s Community Guidelines. Please make sure to familiarize yourself with these as your reservation may be canceled if your production plan is in violation. We also ask for every video produced at YouTube Space to go through a legal clearance review prior to publishing. This entails submitting your unpublished video for our team to confirm it’s free and clear of any legal or copyright claims. Don’t worry though, we explain all of this in detail during Unlock the Space.

  • How can I keep track of the latest events and workshops?

    We are always offering experiences that will help you grow, meet others in the community, and just stay inspired. The best way to stay up-to-date is to sign-up for our newsletter where we always send the latest opportunities available. You can also visit your city's page to check out the events calendar for upcoming events and workshops.

  • How often is the newsletter sent?

    We prefer not to clog your inbox, so we send our newsletter whenever we have events, workshops or production programs that you need to know about. This is usually once a month, but if it’s a busy month you could hear from us twice.

  • What is a Pop-up?

    Pop-up Spaces closely resemble our permanent Spaces providing you with opportunities to create content and attend platform and production workshops. We also will often provide a community lounge space for Coworking or socialising with other creators and artists visiting. You can also always expect other unique opportunities that are specific to the YouTube community of the country we are visiting.

  • How can I attend a Pop-up?

    If you're eligible to attend a Pop-up Space, you should have either an email invite from a partner manager or a notification within the Creator Studio.

  • Who is eligible to apply/attend?

    Typically creators with at least 10,000 subscribers and no strikes on their channel are eligible, and will have received an invite from their YouTube partner manager.

  • Are all creators with 10,000 subscribers invited?

    Typically creators with at least 10,000 subscribers and no strikes on their channel are eligible, and will have received an invite from their YouTube partner manager.

  • What are the selection criteria?

    As long as you meet the above criteria, space is made available for you. If you know of a Pop-up Space coming to your city, please reach out to your partner manager to make sure they get you all the latest info.

  • What would a typical agenda for a Pop up be?

    We work closely with the local community and our own YouTube teammates working in those cities to customize each Pop-up experience, but you can usually epxect workshops that cover topics such as the YouTube platform, optimising you career with YouTube, as well as production and post production techniques.

  • Where can I find the schedule for a Pop-up I was invited to?

    You should be able to see all the available sessions to attend in the invitation you received from your Partner Manager.

  • Can a Pop-up Space come to my city?

    We're' always thinking about where to go next. We pay close attention to communities that are highly engaged and growing quickly - if that describes you and your community let us know here.

  • How much does it cost to attend a Pop-up?

    Our Pop-up Spaces, like all YouTube Spaces, are free to use and attend.

  • How long are Pop-up Spaces?

    Our Pop-up Spaces are usually between three and five days in length.

  • Is my brand channel eligible to take Unlock the Space?

    Brands with more than 10,000 channel subscribers are eligible to Unlock the Space

  • What are the different brand programs that we have available at YouTube Space?

    We offer tours, creative content workshops, and events at YouTube Space catered for selected brands interested in investing in YouTube and working with YouTube creators.

  • How can I get my brand involved with a YouTube Space?

    We currently offer programings to brands with a Google sales representative. Please contact your sales representative for further questions on using the Space.
